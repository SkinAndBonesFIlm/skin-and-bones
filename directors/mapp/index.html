ABOUT

Mapp

Not many directors have won a Grand Prix in Cannes in their past lives as an agency creatives, but Steve Mapp has. Over the years Steve has been a few different things, art director, Creative Director and now just Director. A vigorously trained and tested creative mind, Steve knows what it takes to make the best work in the world. He’s always honing his craft by tackling a wide variety of projects, all with a cinematic approach.
